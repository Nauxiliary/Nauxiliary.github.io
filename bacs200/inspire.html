Oskar Schindler

Oskar Schindler was a member of the Nazi party who made a fortune off of Jewish labour during WWII, only to later spend that fortune saving the lives of 1200 of his workers and ending up penniless.

His selfless sacrifice is truly inspiring to me and I hope I can have the same strength he displayed if I am ever put to the test.
